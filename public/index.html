Slipping words - disintegrate

why do we treat digital information as some thing absolute and eternal.

infact, digital information is one of the most fragile and incomplete form of memory.

this place is designed to let you know that nothing digital is forever and just like the scribble on your note book it can fade into pixels.

here are some simple rules for the project.

  • Anybody can freely type anything you want
  • the words will gradually disintergrate
  • personal attachment with digital files
